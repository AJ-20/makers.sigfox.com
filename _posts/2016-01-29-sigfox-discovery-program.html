SIGFOX Discovery Program

Back in 2013, SIGFOX started to offer its ecosystem partners a way to meet with its sales and technical teams during a so called “SIGFOX Partner Training Days”. In 2015, the attendance of this monthly event jumped by almost 320% compared to its launch year! But it’s not a reason to rest on its laurels!

In this regards SIGFOX is introducing a new SIGFOX Discovery Program to meet a greater demand and to boost IoT projects. The SIGFOX Discovery Program is divided into two main parts:

  • The Entry Level is the perfect first contact with SIGFOX for business minded people;

  • The Advanced Level is focusing on technical topics to help those who whant to design and make a connected device able to communicate efficiently through SIGFOX network.

In addition, Entry Level sessions are moving to Paris, starting on the 03/22/2016 at Renaissance Hotel Paris La Defense to meet the increasing demand and offer more covenient transport options.  Advanced level sessions will still be delivered in the heart of SIGFOX headquarters in Toulouse whereas distance learning options will be shortly introduced.
