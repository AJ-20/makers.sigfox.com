THE SIGFOX MAKERS TOUR HAS ALREADY STARTED!

The first edition of the SIGFOX Makers Tour kicked off in Lille, France on the 8th October in partnership with Euratechnologies. This is the first stop for a programme that will last several months visiting numerous European cities.

Initial feedback has already been extremely positive and an inspiration for what is yet to come during the rest of the tour.

The Tour places SIGFOX in the hands of developers and makers, so that they experience first-hand the possibilities afforded by the SIGFOX network and the added value that it can bring to their projects. These developers are an important community that SIGFOX is proud to support. Not only will they will feed the future IoT market but they might also just be the next company to deploy another million objects on the SIGFOX network !

The first event invited over 30 participants to discover more about SIGFOX through a series of presentations and demos, as well as some dedicated workshops using the Arduino-Akeru development board. We were impressed by the diversity of the profiles of participants which included, students, start-ups, technical staff from large corporate entities, consultants and researchers, all with the same goal to learn more about how SIGFOX is enabling the IoT on a global scale.

We will keep you updated soon with more news from the Tour and details of some of the amazing results and projects being cooked up during the workshop sessions.
