Opening of candidate applications for Le Connected Camp, the IOT industry accelerator

Recognising the challenges inherent in the “Internet Of Things”, the heavy industrialization process as well the vital need to connect startups with large corporations, the IOT Valley launches “Le Connected Camp”, an accelerator for startups in the IOT industry. Applications for the 1st season are now open!

LE CONNECTED CAMP?

“Le Connected Camp” is the accelerator of the IOT Valley, specialising in the IOT industry.

A jury of members and partners of the IOT Valley will select 9 teams. Every entrepreneur integrated to “Le Connected Camp” will, throughout the 9-month program, benefit from hands-on guidance and support from business and technical experts and from corporate partners. These mentors stand out because of their expertise and their network but also because they have the urge to convey the entrepreneurial culture.

The perks of “Le Connected Camp”:

-  An IOT lab dedicated to entrepreneurs

-  An on-site ecosystem of corporate partners


A NEW APPROACH

The selected teams for the 1st season of the program will take advantage of the dynamic environment and relationship opportunities afforded by the IOT Valley: meeting with clients and partners of the ecosystem’s companies, and engaging with IoT valley’s collaborators which constitutes a pool of business and technical experts.

IOT Valley shares the idea and vision that a technology-based company with a unique know- how needs a propitious ecosystem to grow rapidly.

By promoting relationships and exchanges between entrepreneurs and industry, “Le Connected Camp” brings together the skills and expertise indispensable to the rise of new IOT services and applications.


KEY PARTNERS

Experts and partners supporting “Le Connected Camp” are those willing to mobilise and federate all relevant stakeholders and expertise in the IOT ecosystem, and make the IOT Valley into a national and international showcase for the Internet Of Things.
