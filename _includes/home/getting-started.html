Getting started

Hardware
To start sending messages over the Sigfox network, you need to use a Sigfox-compatible communication module or development kit.
These are available from our partners, using standard radio chipsets.
Coverage
As Sigfox is not made for private networks, you need to be in an area covered by our network.
Please contact us if you're located in a not-yet covered country or area, and we'll do our best to provide you an access to the network quickly.
Service access
Sigfox is a service provider, and you need a valid contract with us or your local Sigfox operator to retrieve the messages you send.
A free contract, with access to the full service, is included with each development kit.
Contact us if you need an access to the service for prototypes built upon modules.
