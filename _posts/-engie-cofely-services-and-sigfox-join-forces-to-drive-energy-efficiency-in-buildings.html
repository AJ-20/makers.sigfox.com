ENGIE, Cofely Services and SIGFOX join forces to drive energy efficiency in buildings

ENGIE M2M

As a global energy player, ENGIE (formerly GDF Suez) is present in more than 70 countries and is undoubtedly at the forefront of the many important changes and energy challenges that the world is facing today. Through its subsidiaries, ENGIE is able to provide specific solutions to improve our access to energy, meet changing needs and limit our environmental impact on a planetary scale.

ENGIE believes that the IoT will open up new fields of development and SIGFOX is thrilled to announce that ENGIE M2M will deploy the SIGFOX network dedicated to the Internet of Things (IoT) across Belgium.

With this partnership Belgium will become the seventh European country where the SIGFOX network is being rolled-out. ENGIE M2M will work with Belgian developers and businesses to develop and grow the IoT ecosystem and provide connectivity to customers.

Gérard Mestrallet, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of ENGIE explained that they wanted to establish a strong position in the Internet of Things industry. The creation of “ENGIE M2M”, which represents a 4M€ investment, also demonstrates their commitment to Belgium, which is an historical market for the Group.

The deployment of the SIGFOX network will bring scalable IoT and low-cost Machine-to-Machine (M2M) communication services and open up new frontiers for utilities and smart technologies.

 

Cofely Services

This was also the occasion to announce the new partnership between SIGFOX and Cofely Services - a subsidiary of ENGIE and a European leader in energy efficiency and environment services.

Cofely have chosen to integrate SIGFOX’s connectivity solution to expand the services that they provide for smart buildings and enhance its services for building management. Within three years, over 100,000 objects should be connected, enabling the Group to save energy and develop new services to make buildings smarter and more efficient.

This is perfectly aligned with Cofely’s vision of providing solutions to their Customers based on three pillars of sustainable development :

·      Environmental - through local renewable energy production, energy performance improvements and performance guarantees and CO2 emissions reductions.

·      Economic - with reduced energy bills and more cost-effective energy choices, and,

·      Social - through its social and societal commitments and support of local job creation and job market integration.

Energy efficiency of buildings is an important factor in the struggle against global warming and Cofely opens a new era in driving energy efficiency of buildings and smart metering by connecting its service offering to the SIGFOX energy-efficient, cost-effective network.

Cofely Services has a vast experience in providing sustainable solutions to enable its customers to reduce energy consumption, improve economic performance, and control their environmental impact. However, until today, existing networks or connectivity solutions did not fully answer the needs of the IoT, in terms of energy consumption and cost.

With the adoption of the SIGFOX network, connectivity for the IoT market is clearly democratized, allowing Cofely Services to serve an estimated 400, 0000 customers within the next 3 years, as CEO of Cofely services, Jean-Pierre Monéger said : “With SIGFOX, we open the field of possibilities. Gains in terms of installation and implementation are indeed significant, allowing us to offer our services to new buildings, multi-sites, or buildings that are difficult to access.

Another advantage of using this technology, and based on the expertise of Cofely Services for data processing and quality control, is the ability to conduct smart data processing that will send consumption data and analyze it in real time to improve continually our services”.
