Startup program

Building hardware is a hard process, especially for young and small companies. In order to facilitate this process, SIGFOX built its very own startup program to facilitate this journey.

By joining this program you will get access to a wide range of tools, but also be part of a community of people building amazing products based on the SIGFOX technology.

In order to reach even more brilliant startups, we also partner with the best hardware incubators in the world. To know more about our incubator program, please go here.

By registering to this program, you will get access to :

  • Free network subscription while prototyping (up to 15)
  • Free access to all the SIGFOX learning programs
  • Marketing & communication kit regarding the SIGFOX network
  • Introduction by a SIGFOX expert
  • Access to a wide range of partners offers. From PCB priting to Cloud solutions, you'll enjoy the treat.
  • Access to the SIGFOX builders community trough slack and physical events
  • Subscribe to the entrepreneur newsletter to be updated on all the latest news which could matter to you.

If you're interested to join our program, register here and you will receive all the details about it.

This program is limited to startups founded less than 4 years ago and that raised less than 2 million euros.
